Before you donate money, verify

We gather what people are sharing and cross-check it against each site. Even so, after a disaster, fake campaigns and accounts appear. Prefer organizations with a track record and registration; if one asks for money and doesn't show who's behind it, confirm before giving. We say it plainly on every card below.

Engineering & architecture

Is your building safe?

Tools to assess structural damage. None replaces an official in-person inspection, but they help you get oriented fast.

SismoAyuda VE

Volunteer engineers and architects
Volunteer

You upload photos of the damage and a team of engineers (diaspora, by Hacha y Machete) gives you a preliminary habitability assessment per ATC-20 and EMS-98 standards. Free, no sign-up.

It's a preliminary remote assessment: it doesn't replace an official in-person inspection. The final call is the authorities'.
Report a building →

Mapa de Daños Venezuela

terremotovenezuela.com
Unverified

A quick map and search to document and locate reported damage across the country.

It doesn't identify who runs it or claim any official affiliation. Use it as a reference, not a verified source.
Visit site →

Pilar VZLA

Structural assessment · self-triage
Volunteer

You answer a few questions about your home and get a traffic-light result (green / yellow / red) with a preliminary assessment per ATC-20 criteria. Some cases are reviewed by engineers.

It's a preliminary self-assessment: it doesn't replace inspection by a structural engineer.
